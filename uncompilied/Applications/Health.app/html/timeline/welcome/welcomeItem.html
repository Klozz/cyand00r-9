Track activity.

The Health Mate app offers a basic activity tracking function that uses your iPhone's sensors.
The Withings Pulse lets you go further - it tracks steps, elevation, distance, calories burned, running/walking, sleep cycles, duration and quality. It also lets you measure your instant heart rate and blood oxygen level.

Manage your weight.

Leverage the Healthy Tips and Reminders located in the left side menu to master your body's weight. Set a weight goal and weigh in regularly: research has shown that you are more likely to lose weight when you do it.
The Withings Smart Body Analyzer lets you go further - it measures and automatically sends your weight, BMI and body composition to the Health Mate app. It even monitors indoor air quality.

Compete with friends.

Your friends have an iPhone? Invite them to your Leaderboard, located in the left side menu, to start a weekly steps challenge, have fun and reach the recommended 10.000 daily steps.

Sleep better.

Having trouble falling asleep? Use our Healthy Tips and Reminders to help you adopt good habits.
Use a Withings Pulse to measure sleep quality and duration, in order to have more restorative nights.

Strengthen your heart.

Use our Healthy Tips and Reminders to keep a strong heart. Log your blood pressure and heart rate readings using the "+" button in the upper right hand corner, and share them with your family or doctor.
The Withings Wireless Blood Pressure Monitor eases the process - it measures your BP and heart rate, and automatically syncs the readings to your account.
