What makes my butterfly grow or shrink?

The healthier your lifestyle is, the more your butterfly will grow.

Your weight wing will grow if you weigh-in regularly and move closer to your desired weight.

Your activity wing will grow with each new activity you log.

Your heart wing will grow if you measure your BP regularly, preferably around the same time of the day, and if you tend toward a healthy blood pressure.

Your sleep wing will reach its maximum size if you sleep 7 to 9 hours each night.
