How can I lower my blood pressure?

The best way to lower blood pressure is to change some daily habits. Changes that help reduce blood pressure consist in:

  • Losing weight or maintaining a healthy weight
  • - Adopting a healthy diet
  • - Reducing salt intake
  • - Exercising regularly
  • - Reducing alcohol consumption.
However, people suffering from high blood pressure must often take medication. For more information, consult your doctor.
