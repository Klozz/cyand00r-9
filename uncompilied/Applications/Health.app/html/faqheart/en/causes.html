What are the most frequent causes of hypertension?

There are several factors that contribute to hypertension:

  • Age: arterial pressure increases naturally with age.
  • Gender: arterial pressure is higher for men than for women up to age 50.
  • Heredity: There is a genetic predisposition to hypertension.
  • Diet: Excess consumption of salt, alcohol and coffee are causes of hypertension.
  • Weight: there is a strong correlation between body mass index and arterial pressure.
  • Liquorice derivatives, cocaine, anabolic substances
  • Certain medications
  • A few rare diseases affecting the kidneys, renal arteries and adrenal glands can be directly responsible for hypertension.
