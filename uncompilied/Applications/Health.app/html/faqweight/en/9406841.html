What is a normal body composition?

The normal body composition fulfills the following objectives:

- To have and maintain a weight within limits that minimize health risks

(corresponding to a BMI between 18 and 25),

- Have a muscular mass adapted to your lifestyle,

- Retain enough body fat to ensure it can play its vital role in the body,

but without excess,

- Feel personal satisfaction with your body's aesthetics.

Separately follow the evolutions of fat mass and lean mass and compare the values in relation to references established depending on age, height and gender, helps detect a possible unbalance and understand the reasons for it.

It is essential to refer to a specialist physician if you have any doubts.
