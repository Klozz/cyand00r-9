Why control your fat mass?

Fat is a vital element ensuring the thermal regulation of the body and protecting our organs and joints.

While a certain quantity is therefore necessary to be in good health, excess fat is to be avoided because it leads to health risks, beyond aesthetic consequences. Excess of fat tissue can lead to cardio-vascular or hypertension diseases.

The initial appreciation of the importance of the fat mass and monitoring its evolution during a diet is therefore interesting. An excess of fat isn't always visible and furthermore it isn't always accompanied by excess weight.

During a diet, it is motivating to be able to notice a real loss of fat mass thanks to your efforts.
