How should I take my blood pressure?

  1. Sit comfortably
  2. Rest for around five minutes
  3. Wrap the band around your arm. The aluminium tube should be positioned against the inner part of your upper arm, around 2 cm above your elbow.
  4. Place your arm on a table at heart level.
  5. When you are set, begin the measurement. While inflating and deflating the armband, stay still and relaxed.
