The basics of sleep

Discover why sleep is important and how to improve it.


What is sleep? Why do we need to sleep?

Sleep is something that you spend one third of your life doing. It is a vital bodily function, like breathing or digestion. It plays an important role in learning, memorization and adaptability. It is also essential to your overall well-being.

When should I go to bed?

The urge to go to bed is usually felt at night, around the same time every day. You begin to feel tired or cold, or experience a decrease in mental activity. If you go to bed at that time, you will fall asleep quickly, usually in under ten minutes.


How long do we need to sleep in order to be well rested?

We are all very different when it comes to sleep. Most people need 7 1/2 to 8 hours of sleep. But some people are "short sleepers" who only need 6 hours of sleep a night or less. On the other hand, some people are "long sleepers" who need at least 9 hours of sleep a night.


How do you know if you've slept well?

There is only one way of defining a good night's sleep: it is when you wake up feeling that you have slept well, and that you are well rested and full of energy.
