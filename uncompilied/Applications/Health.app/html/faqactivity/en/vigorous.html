What is a vigorous activity?

It requires a large amount of effort and causes rapid breathing and a substantial increase in heart rate. Examples of vigorous-intensity activity include:

  • Running
  • Walking or climbing briskly up a hill
  • Fast cycling
  • Aerobics
  • Fast swimming;
  • Competitive sports and games (e.g. traditional games, football, volleyball, hockey, basketball...)
  • Heavy shovelling or digging ditches
  • Carrying/moving heavy loads (>20kg).

Source : World Health Organization
