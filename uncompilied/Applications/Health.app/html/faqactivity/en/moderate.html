What is a moderate activity?

It requires a moderate amount of effort and noticeably accelerates the heart rate. Examples of moderate-intensity activity include:

  • Brisk walking
  • Dancing
  • Gardening
  • Housework and domestic chores
  • Traditional hunting and gathering
  • Active involvement in games and sports with children or walking domestic animals
  • General building tasks (e.g. roofing, thatching, painting);
  • Carrying/moving moderate loads (<20kg).

Source : World Health Organization
