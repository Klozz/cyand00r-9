Smart Kid Scale automatically recognizes the baby or child weighted on the basis of his last known weight. If the recognition fails, the scale will display "Guest" at the top of its screen:

In such case, the weight reading will appear in the "Unknown measures" pane of the Baby Companion app. To ensure a proper recognition in the future, please manually assign the measure to the right profile by visiting the "Unknown measures" pane in the app main menu.

We hope you'll enjoy your Smart Kid Scale. Any additional question or issue, please visit our support page at: http://support.withings.com.
