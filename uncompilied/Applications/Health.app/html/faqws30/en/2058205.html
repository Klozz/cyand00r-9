What device or software do I need to use the Wireless Scale?

You must own an iPhone (5, 4s, 4, 3GS), iPad (all versions) or iPod touch (3G, 4G, 5G) running iOS 5 or newer. You will need to install the free Withings Health Mate app on it to set up the Wireless Scale. Your iPhone, iPad or iPod touch must have access to the Internet (by Wi-Fi or 3G).
